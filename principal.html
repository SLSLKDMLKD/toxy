Welcome to Pimburuwellegama College

Message from the Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Pimburuwellegama College, an institution that has been at the forefront of educational excellence for many years. Our school stands as a beacon of knowledge, character development, and holistic growth.

At PCC, we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also developing well-rounded individuals who will contribute meaningfully to society. Our dedicated team of teachers works tirelessly to provide quality education while instilling strong values and ethics in our students.

We focus on creating an environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. Our curriculum is designed to meet the challenges of the modern world while preserving our rich cultural heritage. We provide numerous opportunities for our students to explore their talents through various academic and extra-curricular activities.

As we move forward, we remain committed to our vision of creating future leaders who are knowledgeable, compassionate, and ready to face the challenges of tomorrow. I invite you to be part of our journey in shaping the future of our nation.

Mr. D.M. Jayawardena
Principal
Pimburuwellegama College
