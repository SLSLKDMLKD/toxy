Secondary Education

Grades 6-11: Building Strong Foundations for Success

Secondary Education at PCC

Our secondary education program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive learning experience that prepares them for higher education and future careers. We focus on developing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and character development.

The curriculum follows the national education guidelines while incorporating innovative teaching methods and modern technology to enhance the learning experience. Our experienced teachers are dedicated to helping each student reach their full potential.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering all core subjects
  • Modern teaching methods and technology integration
  • Regular assessments and progress monitoring
  • Extra-curricular activities and sports programs
  • Career guidance and counseling services

Core Subjects

Mathematics

Advanced mathematics including algebra, geometry, and statistics to develop strong analytical skills.

Science

Comprehensive coverage of physics, chemistry, and biology with practical laboratory work.

Languages

Sinhala, English, and Tamil language studies focusing on literature and communication.

Social Studies

History, geography, and civics to understand our world and heritage.

ICT

Computer science and information technology skills for the digital age.

Aesthetics

Art, music, and dance to nurture creativity and cultural appreciation.

Our Facilities

Science Laboratories

Modern laboratories equipped with the latest equipment for physics, chemistry, and biology experiments.

Computer Labs

State-of-the-art computer facilities with high-speed internet for ICT education.

Library

Extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources for research and reading.

Sports Facilities

Well-maintained sports grounds and facilities for various sports and physical education.
